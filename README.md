## Description

Imagine transforming your 3D printing experience with a DIY enclosure upgrade that costs just $100. This transforms your enclosure into a temperature-controlled and air-filtered system, ensuring your prints come out perfect every time, and your lungs breath cleaner air. Whether you're a seasoned maker or a curious beginner, this project will walk you through the steps to create a professional-grade enclosure without breaking the bank.
